Translation of party – English–Vietnamese dictionary

party

 
noun
  /ˈpaːti/  
plural parties
Add to word list Add to word list
●
a meeting of guests for entertainment, celebration etc
bữa tiệc
a birthday party
She’s giving/having a party tonight
(also adjective) a party dress.
●
a group of people with a particular purpose
nhóm
a party of tourists.
●
a group of people with the same ideas and purposes, especially political
đảng
a political party.

party

 
verb
 
●
to enjoy yourself by eating, drinking, dancing etc with other people
tiệc tùng
She was out all night partying again.

(Translation of party from the PASSWORD English-Vietnamese Dictionary © 2015 K Dictionaries Ltd)
